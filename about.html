Project Overview


This Final Extravaganza project has me create a website from scratch, using design elements from a past rebranding project for Roast Coffee Company. The goal is to make a fancy and luxurious browsing experience for a website about searching and purchasing high-quality and pricey coffee with a royalty motif.


This is a functional site with a home page, a shopping page, a brewing index, and an about page, all of which help the user navigate and find the coffee which they are looking for. The use of black and gold will highlight the fancy feel and overall sleek finish, and help the site look alive and genuine.


Designer Bio


Christopher Monroy is a designer based in Wisconsin. He is skilled in various forms of design such as illustration, UI, web design, motion graphics, and more.
